LOCATION HOTEL

This quiet beachfront hotel is located on Timsah Lake, 3 km from the Suez Canal and 118 km from Cairo International Airport.

The warm rooms have balconies overlooking the lake or the garden, as well as minibars, air conditioning, flat-screen TVs and free Wi-Fi.Room service is available 24/7.

The hotel has free parking.There is also an outdoor swimming pool, a private beach, a sauna and a gym, as well as gardens, sports fields and a children's playground.

Other amenities include 2 bars (one by the pool), 3 simple restaurants, as well as space for meetings and events.

Breakfast is served (at an additional cost).
